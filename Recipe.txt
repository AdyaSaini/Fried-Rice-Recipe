Take a pan and put it on gas (on medium flame).
Then put Olive oil.    
After a minute add mustard seeds, chopped onion, chopped chillis and ginger to it.
After sautéing them for 1-2 min add all the vegetables and spices, keep sautéing till they are soft and cooked now take the rice (make sure the rice has cooled down and is not hot) and add it to the vegetables.
Mix properly.
Your dish is ready to serve.
